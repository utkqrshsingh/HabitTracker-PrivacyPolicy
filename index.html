1. Introduction

Welcome to Habit Tracker ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information: When you sign up or create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Google Account information (if using Google Sign-In)

Habit Data: Information about habits you create and track:

  • Habit names and descriptions
  • Habit categories
  • Completion logs and timestamps
  • Streak information
  • Personal notes and observations

AI Coach Interactions:

  • Messages you send to our AI Coach
  • Chat history and conversations
  • Responses from the AI system

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Mobile network information

App Usage Data:

  • Features you access
  • Time spent in the app
  • Actions performed
  • Crash reports and error logs

Location Information (if enabled):

  • Approximate location based on IP address
  • ❌ We do NOT collect precise GPS location

2.3 Third-Party Information

Google Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your Google account information
  • Email address
  • Profile information

📱 To Provide Services

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your habit data is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Account information and chat history are stored on secure servers
  • Database: We use Firebase and Room Database for data persistence

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • ✅ Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • ✅ SSL/TLS: All communications use secure protocols
  • ✅ Authentication: Passwords are hashed and encrypted
  • ✅ Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access your data
  • ✅ Regular Audits: We conduct regular security assessments
⚠️ Important: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data

❌ We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share limited information with:

  • Google: For authentication services (Google Sign-In)
  • Firebase: For app analytics and crash reporting
  • Google Cloud: For data storage and processing
  • Gemini AI: Your chat messages are processed by Google's Generative AI API

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Prevent fraud or criminal activity
  • Protect public health and safety

5.4 Business Transitions

If our company is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access Your Data

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Review your habit history and AI conversations

6.2 Correct Your Data

You can update or correct your account information at any time through the app settings.

6.3 Delete Your Data

You have the right to request deletion of:

  • Your account and all associated data
  • Your chat history with AI Coach
  • Your habit logs and personal information

6.4 Opt-Out Options

You can disable:

  • Push notifications for habit reminders
  • Location services
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • AI Coach interactions

6.5 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what data we collect
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to opt-out of data sales
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

For users between 13-18, parental consent is recommended.
